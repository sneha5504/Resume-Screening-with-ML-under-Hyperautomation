# Resume-Screening-with-ML-under-Hyperautomation
Using Machine Learning to automatically analyze, rank, and shortlist resumes. Integrates OCR, NLP, and predictive models to match candidates with job requirements.

Hyperautomation Overview: 
-Application of advanced technologies like AI, ML, and RPA.
-Enables end-to-end automation of complex business processes.
-Enhances efficiency, accuracy, and decision-making.

Focus on Resume Screening: Hyperautomation combines OCR, NLP, and ML to:
-Extract information from resumes.
-Analyze and rank candidates.
-Automate responses and shortlisting.

Problem Statement: Time-consuming due to high volume of applicants.
-Prone to human error and bias.
-Inconsistent evaluations across recruiters.

The Importance of Hyperautomation:
-Efficiency Gains: Streamlines workflows, reduces manual effort, lowers costs, and speeds up task execution.
-Improved Accuracy: Minimizes human errors, enhances data consistency, and delivers more reliable outcomes.
-Scalability: Enables organizations to expand operations quickly and handle growing workloads with ease.
-Strategic Value: Frees up employees from repetitive tasks, allowing focus on innovation, strategy, and creative problem-solving.

Use Case: Resume Screening
Problem: Screening large volumes of resumes is time-consuming and resource-intensive.
-Prone to human errors, inconsistencies, and unconscious bias.
-Leads to delays in hiring and risk of overlooking qualified candidates.

Solution: Machine Learning (ML) to automatically analyze, rank, and shortlist resumes.
-Integrates OCR, NLP, and predictive models to match candidates with job requirements.

Benefits:
-Faster screening
-Reduced bias
-Increased recruiter productivity

Conclusion:
-Showcased the potential of hyperautomation in HR.
-Demonstrated the use of ML for automated resume screening.

Future Work: 
-Parsing resumes from PDFs
-Skill-weighted scoring for more accurate candidate ranking.

Summary:
-Successfully automated the resume screening process by extracting, analyzing, and shortlisting candidate data from Excel sheets.
-Evaluated profiles based on experience, skills, and education, significantly reducing manual HR effort and accelerating hiring decisions.
